LinkML SchemasÔÉÅ

This introduces the key parts of LinkML that you need to know to create a schema in order to model your data.

Note that the formal specification for LinkML can be found at https://w3id.org/linkml/specification. This formal specification is aimed at implementors and not users. General users of LinkML should use the guide below.
